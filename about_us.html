ABOUT US

Welcome to CLOTHING. The motivation that started us in 2009 is the same today - helping you.To all who have stuck by our side since day one - we thank you. To our next friend to experience CLOTHING and join us on the walk, we’re looking forward to adventuring together.
At CLOTHING we make holistically designed technical wear, inspired by our adventures, built to adapt to whatever weather, challenge, activity or terrain you are facing. But let’s face it, that’s a mouth full; what does that really mean?
